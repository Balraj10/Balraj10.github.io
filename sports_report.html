Sports Report

NBA

Adding To An Ever-Growing Legacy

Kobe Bryant added another accomplishment to his prominent resume: The Oscar. After an illustrous NBA career, spanning two decades, Kobe Bryant continues to find success, but this time it was of the court. Kobe Bryant took home an Academy Award in the animated short film category on Sunday for his work 'Dear Basketball', which he made as the executive producer. 'Dear Basketball' journeys through Kobe Bryant's basketball career, starting with his dream to play in the NBA as a child and venturing into his stardom with the Los Angeles Lakers. 'Dear Basketball' is based on the poem Kobe Bryant had written in 2015 for the Players' Tribune.

NHL

From An Expansion Team To Title Contenders

The expectations were low for this team, made up of a group of ragtag players that teams didn't want. They were expected to be in rebuild for the forseeable future and yet, they are the second best team in the NHL so far this season.With players such as Marchessault, Peron, Sbisa, and Neal being the key players for this team. When the trade deadline rolled by, the Knights were aggressive, trying to land Karlsson right up until the deadline before finalizing other deals. They brought in Reaves in a three team trade for almost nothing and made their biggest move when they acquired Tatar for three picks.The Knights have only been improving and considering the success they are enjoying now as well as the depth of their team, the Knights have a legitimate chance at winning the Stanely Cup this summer.

Soccer

O Captain, My Captain You Shall Be Missed

Fiorentina soccer captain Davide Astori was discovered dead of cardiac arrest by natural causes on Sunday, just hours before an Italian legue match. The defender was discovered by club staff when he failed to arrive for breakfast at the hotel. In Florence, fans attached scarves, flowers, and messages to the gates of the team's stadium, while many other stadiums also put up banners to pay tribute to him. Many of his past and present teammates reached out and wrote touching tributes on their social media accounts to describe what kind of person Astori was and they found no better word than a comforting friend.

Boxing

Joining Elite Company

After Saturday night's win over Luis Ortiz in the 10th round, not only did Deontay Wilder retain his WBC world heavyweight boxing championship, but he also ran his career record to a perfect 40-0. He joins elite company, which consisting of only twelve other boxers who started their careers a perfect 40-0. This includes boxers such as George Foreman, Ricardo Lopez, and Floyd 'Money' Mayweather Jr. After this win Deontay Wilder took the opportunity to call out Anthony Joshua, the current WBA and IBF titleholder, for a possible bout.
